Fruit Salad

Description

A refreshing mix of fresh fruits that's healthy and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 apple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 orange
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 1 tablespoon honey(optional)

Steps

  1. Wash all fruits.
  2. Cut the apple, banana, and orange into small pieces.
  3. Add all fruits to a bowl.
  4. Dizzle honey on top if desired.
  5. Mix gently and serve.
