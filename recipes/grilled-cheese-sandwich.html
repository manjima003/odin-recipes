Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Grilled cheese sandwiches are a simple and delicious comfort food. They are crispy on the outside and filled with melted cheese on the inside.

This recipe requires only a few ingredients and can be prepared in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the bread slices.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot.
